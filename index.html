Welcome to the tiles memory game!

What is a memory game?

The memory game is a series of objects which have matching pairs. You may only reveal 2 object at a time as they will initially be hidden. If the two objects match, you can then proceed in trying to find the next matching pair. If both objects do not match, then you must hide the objects once again but try to remember where they are for later. Once you have found all matching pairs, you will have completed the game.

How do i play?

If you are feeling up to the challenge of a short yet fun memory game, select one of the difficulty levels below. Once re-directed to the memory game page, you will be faced with a number of plain tiles. Click one of the tiles whenever you are ready to begin! Easy difficulty will serve 12 cards to you with 750 miliseconds to memorise where they were if you fail to match a pair. Medium difficulty will serve you 16 cards with only 450 milliseconds to memorise their positions. Hard difficulty will serve 20 cards to you with only 225 milliseconds to memories their placement on the deck! Once all cards have their matching pair selected, you will be notified that you have beaten the level. Good luck!
